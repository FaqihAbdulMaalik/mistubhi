Phase 03 — Fuel Your Body
Nutrition
With What You Have

You have eggs, chicken, rice, and milk — that's actually a GREAT foundation. Here's how to maximize what's at home for muscle growth and skin health.

🥚
Eggs
Complete protein source. Eat 3–4 eggs daily. Boiled is best for muscle building. Yolk contains B12, Vitamin D, and biotin — all crucial for skin and hair.
Eat Daily
🍗
Chicken
Lean protein for muscle recovery. Eat post-workout when possible. Boil or grill — avoid frying. 150–200g chicken breast per serving is ideal for muscle growth.
Post Workout
🍚
Rice
Main carb source — gives energy for training. Eat more on training days, less on rest days. Brown rice if available gives longer-lasting energy.
Energy Source
🥛
Milk
Calcium + protein combo. Great for bone density and muscle recovery. Drink 1–2 glasses daily. Best before bed or post-workout for recovery benefits.
Before Sleep
🥦
Vegetables
Even a small portion of spinach, kale, or any veggie adds fiber and vitamins. Hugely helps with skin brightening and digestion.
Add When Possible
💧
Water
Minimum 2–3 liters daily. Dehydration causes dull skin and slows muscle growth. Start every morning with 2 glasses before anything else.
2L+ Daily
📋 IDEAL DAILY MEAL PLAN
MORNING (6–7AM) 2 boiled eggs + 1 glass milk + rice
LUNCH (12PM) Rice + chicken + vegetable side
AFTERNOON (3PM) 1–2 boiled eggs (pre-workout snack)
DINNER (7PM) Rice + protein (chicken/eggs) + side dish
BEFORE SLEEP 1 glass warm milk
The Journey
12 Month
Transformation
Month 1–2
Building The Foundation
Build habits. Start skincare routine, consistent water intake, begin training 3–4x a week. Don't expect instant results — you're building foundations. Small wins matter.
Month 3–4
Early Signs
Skin starts clearing up. Body begins reshaping. Energy increases. Others may not notice yet but YOU will feel it. Stay consistent. Add weight to your lifts.
Month 5–6
Visible Changes
People around you start noticing. Clothes fit better. Skin tone evens out. Posture improved. This is where motivation naturally surges — maintain this momentum.
Month 7–9
Major Upgrade
Significant muscle development visible. Skin looks naturally healthy. Style taste sharpens. Confidence rises. This phase is when your real transformation becomes obvious to everyone.
Month 10–12
Your New Self
After 1 year of consistency — better body, clearer skin, improved posture, sharper style. You won't recognize the old you. This is just the beginning — now it's time to level up.
